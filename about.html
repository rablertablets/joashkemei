When in Doubt, Ask for Simba Ni Moja Mentors

The tagline "Simba Ni Moja", which translates to "One Lion", is not just a catchy phrase, but a philosophy Simba holds dearly. Joash believes that in the jungle of life, standing strong and having self-confidence, like a lion, is essential. However, even the lion doesn't survive without a pack and that is where the concept of mentorship comes in.

It’s about creating a community that supports each other while building individual strength.

Simba's encourages people to step away from the fear of failure and the pressure of society’s expectations. Instead, it teaches the importance of embracing one's true calling by instilling the belief in career success. Through understanding who you are, what drives you, and how you can make a meaningful impact in the society we live in.

Mentorship: A Key to Success

In today’s fast-paced world, finding a mentor who not only inspires but also guides you to success is priceless. Joash is a beacon of hope and wisdom to many striving to shape their destiny. We are dedicated and equal to the task of empowering individuals to reach their full potential through comprehensive coaching, training, counseling, and academic excellence programs.

Personal Testimony on Mentorship

I was lucky to have gone to a high school that invited many people to talk to us. One of the things they emphasized was getting a mentor if we desired success in life. That was when I started writing in my notebook about life’s priorities, and getting a mentor was one of them.

Looking back on my university career in Science, I have realized that one key to success is mentorship. This is especially true as a man in science—we stand on the shoulders of those before us. I have been extraordinarily lucky to have fantastic mentorship throughout my studies, which has also translated into my non-professional life.

Who is Joash Kemei?

Joash Kemei is the founder of Simba Ni Moja Mentors since 2015. He is a renowned corporate trainer, academic mentor, brand influencer, and adults/youth counselor. His programs have impacted millions worldwide, touching on academic excellence, team building, and career advancement.

He studied at Kaplelmet Primary in Nandi County, later excelling at Samoei Boys Secondary. At university, Joash pursued a BSc. Food Nutrition and Dietetics at South Eastern Kenya University and is currently a Master’s student in Applied Human Nutrition at the University of Nairobi.

He is a mentor. Reach him to talk to groups of students, youth, adults, and teams. Leave no doubts!
